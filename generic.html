STEAM Week 2020: Nov 30th-Dec 4th

STEAM Week is a week-long event (the week after Thanksgiving break) full of workshops, accomplished guest speakers, and opportunities for you to gain valuable exposure in any STEAM field!! From 11/30 to 12/04 everyday at 4:00 PM, Superposition will be presenting 1.5 hours-long sessions for you to learn from an expert on the subject of your choice. Each day is a different subject, and YOU get to choose which ones you'd like to attend! Our speakers include the Director of the NASA Ames Research Center and recipient of the Presidential Rank Award: the esteemed Dr. Eugene Tu, who will be talking about his work in aeronautical engineering; as well as MIT alumni and mathematician at Lawrence Livermore National Labs, Carol Meyers. Sign up for STEAM Week HERE (or down below!) to hear from them and many other inspiring speakers!
